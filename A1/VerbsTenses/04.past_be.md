### **Past Simple: to be (Was / Were)**

**Quick Map**
* I / He / She / It -> was
* You / We / They -> were

**Structure**
* Plus: I was tired. / They were late.
* Minus: She wasn't ready. / We weren't there.
* Ask: Were you at the meeting? / Was it helpful?

**Noticeable Feedback**
* The "You" Rule: "You" always takes were, even for one person.
  * Incorrect: You was right -> Correct: You were right
* Group Nouns: Words like team or family take was (US) or were (UK). Stay consistent.
