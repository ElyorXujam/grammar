### **Present Simple (Habits & Facts)**

**Structure**
* Plus: 
  * I / You / We / They -> work
  * He / She / It -> works (Always add -s)
* Minus: Use don't / doesn't + Base Verb
  * She doesn't work here. (Not: doesn't works)
* Ask: Use Do / Does + Base Verb
  * Does he know the goal? (Not: Does he knows)

**Noticeable Feedback**
* The "-s" Trap: Forgetting the third-person -s is highly noticeable. Fix: He speaks, It helps.
* State Verbs: Verbs like know, believe, understand don't use "-ing". Keep them here: I understand you.
