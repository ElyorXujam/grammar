### **Present Continuous (Now / Temporary)**

**Structure**
am / is / are + Verb-ing

* Plus: We're updating the code right now.
* Minus: I'm not using that laptop today.
* Ask: Are they testing the system?

**Present Simple vs. Continuous**
* Routine: I work from home. (Always true)
* Now: I am working from home today. (Temporary)

**Noticeable Feedback**
* Missing "to be": Don't drop the helper verb.
  * Incorrect: They working -> Correct: They are working
* State Verbs: Never say "I am wanting" -> Say "I want"
