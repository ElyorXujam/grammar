### **Verb to be (Present)**

**Quick Map**
* I -> am (I'm)
* He / She / It -> is (He's / She's / It's)
* You / We / They -> are (You're / We're / They're)

**Structure**
* Plus: I'm ready. / She is here.
* Minus: I'm not busy. / They aren't available.
* Ask: Are you sure? / Is it working?

**Noticeable Feedback**
* No Double Subjects: Don't say "My manager she is coming." -> Say "My manager is coming."
* Contractions: Use isn't or aren't to sound natural. Rigid text sounds robotic.
